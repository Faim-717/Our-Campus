The Hackathon was designed to create and implement projects to bring people with technical backgrounds together for problem solving and developing new ideas. Hackathons can help participants to grow their IT skills, meet people and work with others in their field.

Participate in the Circular Hackathons

The Hackathon is aimed at teams of students, recent graduates and doctoral students from any University. We are looking for creative candidates who are passionate about circular hackathons and who want to get involved. More information on requirements and methods can be found in the Regulation. Candidates and their teams will have time to present their project ideas from 2 to 31 May 2023.
